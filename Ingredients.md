**Preparation Time:**

Less than 30 minutes

**Cooking Time:**

30 minutes to 1 hour

**Ingredients Required:**

_For the Caramel_
* 1/2 cup of sugar
* 2 tablespoons of water
* Butter (to grease the ramekins)

_For the Custard_
* 1/4 cup sugar
* 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
* 2 cups of hot milk
* 3 lightly beaten eggs
